# ComponentOf

## Description

ComponentOf captures component-functional-complex parthood, in which a part is understood as contributing to the functionality or behavior of a functional complex. The Phase 1 source distinguishes this from Collective membership: while members of a Collective can be treated as playing a uniform role type, components of a functional complex may play multiple role types in the internal organization of the whole.

The source presents component-functional-complex parthood as important for conceptual modeling, but it does not support treating ComponentOf as unconditionally transitive. It notes that identifying the relevant context for a complex may require domain knowledge because the unifying relations may themselves be complex. The same source does, however, support transitivity along chains of essential component parthood: where components are essential to complexes, the chain preserves the relevant functional-dependence and parthood commitments.

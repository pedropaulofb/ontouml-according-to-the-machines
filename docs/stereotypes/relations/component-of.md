# ComponentOf

## Description

ComponentOf is supported in the supplied Phase 1 source contribution through component-functional-complex parthood. In this account, a functional complex is a whole whose components contribute to the functionality or behavior of the whole. The contribution distinguishes this from collective membership: components of a functional complex may play multiple role types within the organization of the whole, rather than a uniform member role type.

The source-specific contribution presents component-functional-complex parthood as important for conceptual modeling, but it does not support treating ComponentOf as unconditionally transitive. It indicates that identifying the relevant contexts for complexes can require domain knowledge because the unifying relations may be complex. The same contribution supports transitivity across chains of essential component parthood: for complexes, essential components entail functional dependence, and chains of essential parthood preserve the relevant dependence and parthood commitments.

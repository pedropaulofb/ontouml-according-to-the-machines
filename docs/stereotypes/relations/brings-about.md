# BringsAbout

## Description

BringsAbout relates an Event to a Situation that results from the Event's occurrence. The resulting Situation obtains at the Event's end point and represents the effects produced by that Event.

One source-specific formal account further characterizes each Event as having a unique maximal Situation that it brings about. Another source treats BringsAbout as a causal or change relation from events to situations, where the brought-about Situation may involve endurants being created, destroyed, changed, or participating in specific ways.

For Phase 1 purposes, BringsAbout should therefore be understood as linking an Event to the Situation resulting from its occurrence, rather than directly to an enduring entity. It is also relevant to source accounts of causation between events: a Situation brought about by one Event may be connected to the occurrence of a further Event, and one source uses the pattern in which an Event brings about a Situation that triggers another Event to define direct causation.

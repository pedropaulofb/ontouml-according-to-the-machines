# Sub-Collection-Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

This source supports `Sub-Collection-Of` through its account of subcollection-collection parthood. The relation holds between collectives, or plural entities constituted by such collectives, when the atoms of the subcollection are also atoms of the larger collection.

The source explains subcollections as arising from refinements of the unifying relation that determines a collection. Under that condition, subcollection parthood is transitive. When fully specified, the relation is constrained to collectives at both ends, and the subcollection is essential to the collection in the modeled sense.

The source also supports propagation from membership through subcollection parthood: if an entity is a member of a subcollection, and that subcollection is part of a collection under the refinement condition, then the entity is also a member of the larger collection.

# Sub Collection Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.3.2 and table 8-4, `subCollectionOf` is a parthood relation between two collectives. The source distinguishes it from `subQuantityOf` by allowing both shareable and non-shareable cases.

The source assigns `subCollectionOf` the meta-properties non-reflexivity, anti-symmetry, transitivity, and weak supplementation. If cardinality constraints are fully specified, the part end has cardinality one and exactly one. The source constrains both connected classifiers to represent universals whose instances are collectives, either directly through `collective` or through specialization compatible with collectives.

# Sub-Quantity-Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

This source supports `Sub-Quantity-Of` through its account of quantity/mass parthood. The relation applies between quantities, understood as objectified portions of matter. Because quantities are treated as maximal self-connected portions and because the relevant spatial containment reasoning preserves transitivity, the source argues that parthood among quantities is always transitive.

The source further characterizes quantity parthood as non-shareable: a quantity part belongs to one maximal quantity whole in the relevant sense. When fully specified, the relation is constrained to quantities at both ends, the part is essential to the whole, and the cardinality at both association ends is exactly one.

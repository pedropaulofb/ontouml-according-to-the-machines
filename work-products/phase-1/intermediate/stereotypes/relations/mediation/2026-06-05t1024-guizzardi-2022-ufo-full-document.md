# Mediation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, mediation connects a relator to the individual that the relator connects in a relational statement. The source formalizes `mediates(x, y)` with `x` as a relator and `y` as an endurant, grounded in a qua individual of `y` that is part of the relator.

The source derives that every relator mediates at least two distinct endurants. In the source's examples and formalizations, mediation is also used as the immutable existential-dependence link from a relator to the mediated endurants.

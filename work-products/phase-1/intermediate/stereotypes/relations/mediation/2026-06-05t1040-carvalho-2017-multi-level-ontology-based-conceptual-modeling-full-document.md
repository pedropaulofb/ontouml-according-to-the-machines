# Mediation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source identifies mediation as the relation connecting a Relator Universal to substantial universals. It is used to make explicit that relators link or depend on the relata classified by the mediated substantial universals (Section 2.1, p. 3).

In second-order modeling, mediation can be specialized to align a taxonomy of relators with a taxonomy of roles. A role type can be categorized according to the type of relator its instances bear, and specialized mediation links the corresponding role and relator types (Section 5.3.3, p. 17).

For Phase 1, this source supports documenting mediation as the relation used to connect role-like classifications to relator classifications, including in higher-order patterns that categorize roles by relator type.

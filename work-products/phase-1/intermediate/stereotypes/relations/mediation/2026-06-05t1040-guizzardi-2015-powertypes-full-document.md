# Mediation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source's contribution to mediation is grounded in the OntoUML-style model and its accompanying explanation of relators. The model uses mediation links between a relator and the role-bearing participants involved in the relational context.

The text characterizes the relevant relator as multiply existentially dependent on the participants. This supports the mediation contribution as the relation that connects a dependent relational entity to the entities or roles it depends on.

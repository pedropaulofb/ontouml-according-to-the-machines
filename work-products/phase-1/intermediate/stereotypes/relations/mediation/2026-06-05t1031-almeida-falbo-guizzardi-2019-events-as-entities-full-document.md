# Mediation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source recalls `<<mediation>>` as the OntoUML relation for multiple existential dependency involving relators. In the source's background model, a relator depends on the endurants it connects; this particular relation is termed mediation in OntoUML.

The source uses `<<mediation>>` to contrast current relationship-based role playing with historical role playing. A current `<<role>>` is associated through `<<mediation>>` to a `<<relator>>`, whereas a `<<historicalRole>>` is associated through `<<participation>>` to an `<<event>>`.

This contribution is limited to mediation as background and contrast for the event extension. The source does not introduce a new mediation stereotype or revise the general mediation profile.

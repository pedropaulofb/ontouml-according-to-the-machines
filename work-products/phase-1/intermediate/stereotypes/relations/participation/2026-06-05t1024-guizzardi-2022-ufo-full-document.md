# Participation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, participation relates endurants to perdurants. The source defines participation through manifestation: an endurant participates in a perdurant when that perdurant has a part that manifests a disposition inhering in the endurant.

The source also states that perdurants are dependent on the substantials that participate in them. This contribution is limited to the participation account explicitly provided by the source.

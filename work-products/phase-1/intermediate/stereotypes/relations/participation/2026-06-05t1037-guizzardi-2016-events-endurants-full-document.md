# Participation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source treats participation as a relation between endurants and events. Events may involve endurants as participants, and the nature of each participation determines which processual role the endurant plays in the event.

Participation is one of the event aspects explicitly treated in the UFO account reported by the source. The source distinguishes endurant participation in events from the endurant-event manifestation relation: participation concerns the involvement of endurants in an event, whereas manifestation concerns the unfolding of particularized properties of endurants.

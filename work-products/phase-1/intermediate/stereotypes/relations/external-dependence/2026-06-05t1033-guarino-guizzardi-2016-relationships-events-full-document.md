# External Dependence

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source supports an External Dependence contribution through its account of existential dependence (pp. 6, 8).
- It characterizes particularized properties and qualities as object-like entities that depend on a bearer and inhere in that bearer (p. 6).
- For extrinsic descriptive relations, at least one quality in the relevant quality complex is relational and existentially dependent on the other relatum or relata; this dependence helps distinguish extrinsic descriptive relations from intrinsic ones (p. 8).
- In this source, such dependencies are part of the weak-truthmaking structure that makes relational propositions true and allows the same reified relationship to change qualitatively over time (pp. 8-9).

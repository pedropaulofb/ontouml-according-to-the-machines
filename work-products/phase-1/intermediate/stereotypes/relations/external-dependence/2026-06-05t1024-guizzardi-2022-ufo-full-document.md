# External Dependence

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, external dependence is used to characterize modes that inhere in one entity while depending on another entity that is independent of the bearer. This is a specialization of existential dependence used for externally dependent modes.

The source uses external dependence to capture truly relational qualities. It also connects externally dependent modes to qua individuals and to material relations, with externally dependent modes serving as truthmakers for one-sided material relations.

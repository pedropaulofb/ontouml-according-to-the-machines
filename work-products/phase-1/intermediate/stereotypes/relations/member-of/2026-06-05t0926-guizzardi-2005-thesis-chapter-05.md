# Member-Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

This source supports `Member-Of` through its account of member-collection parthood. The relation holds between a singular entity and a plural or collective whole. The singular member is treated as atomic relative to the context in which the collection is defined.

The source characterizes this relation as intransitive. If an entity is a member of a collection, its own parts are not thereby members of that collection. This distinguishes membership from both subcollection parthood and quantity parthood, where transitivity conditions are supported under different assumptions.

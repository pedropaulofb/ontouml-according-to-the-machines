# Member Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source uses `memberOf` in its account of qualities and quality structures. `Quale` is an `AbstractIndividual`, `QualityStructure` is a `Set`, and quales are members of quality structures (p. 5, Figure 1; p. 11, Section 3.8; p. 15, axioms a20-a21).

The relation is constrained so that each `Quale` is a member of exactly one `QualityStructure`, while each `QualityStructure` has at least one quale as a member (p. 11, Section 3.8; p. 17, axioms a98, a112). In the source's quality theory, `memberOf` helps constrain the admissible quales for qualities that instantiate a given `QualityUniversal`, because quality structures are associated with quality universals (p. 11-12, Section 3.8, Q2-Q3).

This source-specific contribution is limited to the use of `memberOf` for quales and quality structures; it does not provide a general account of membership for collections or quantities.

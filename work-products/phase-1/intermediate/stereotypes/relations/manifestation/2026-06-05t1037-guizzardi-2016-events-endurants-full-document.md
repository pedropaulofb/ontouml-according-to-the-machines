# Manifestation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source uses manifestation to connect events to the particularized properties of endurants that make those events possible. Events are manifestations of qualities and dispositions; an event unfolds only because the relevant potentiality exists as a concrete property of some endurant.

This relation also grounds the dependence of events on endurants. Since dispositions and qualities depend on endurants, and events manifest those properties, events are dependent on the corresponding endurants. In the structural modeling pattern proposed in the source, events in the life of an endurant are manifestations whose accumulation constitutes the current or final life of that endurant.

# Manifestation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source makes manifestation central to the connection between qualities, relationships, and events (pp. 6, 11).
- A descriptive property can have a corresponding event as the manifestation of the relevant trope or quality (p. 6).
- More generally, events are treated as manifestations of individual qualities (p. 11).
- For relational events, the event is understood as the sum of manifestations of the qualities that constitute the focal relationship; the relationship is the focus, while the event provides the broader spatiotemporal and participant context (pp. 11-12).

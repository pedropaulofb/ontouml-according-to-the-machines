# Manifestation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, manifestation connects endurants and perdurants. The source states that any endurant is connected to a perdurant by the manifestation relation, and it defines the life of an endurant as a perdurant that aggregates the events manifesting it.

Conceptually, perdurants are manifestations of dispositions. The source uses this relation to explain how event occurrence is grounded in underlying endurants and their dispositions, and how apparent event change can be traced to either temporal-part variation or change in the underlying endurant.

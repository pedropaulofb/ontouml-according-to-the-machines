# Brings About

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source treats brings-about as a causal or change relation from events to situations. Events can bring about situations in which endurants are created, destroyed, changed, or participate in specific ways. In the UFO account summarized by the source, causation connects situations brought about by events with further event occurrence.

For Phase 1 purposes, this supports brings-about as the relation that links an event to the situation resulting from its occurrence, rather than to the enduring entity directly.

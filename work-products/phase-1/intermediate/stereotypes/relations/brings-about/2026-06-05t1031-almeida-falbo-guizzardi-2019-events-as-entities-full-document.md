# Brings About

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source situates `brings about` in the UFO-B theory of change. Events map the world from situations that activate dispositions to situations that are brought about by the occurrence of those events.

In the source's causal account, if an event brings about a situation and that situation activates dispositions manifested as another event, then the situation triggers the latter event and the first event causes it.

The source also uses `brings about` in the definition of historical dependence. Historical dependence can hold when an event, or one of its parts, brings about a situation that triggers another event, or brings about a situation that is necessary for such triggering.

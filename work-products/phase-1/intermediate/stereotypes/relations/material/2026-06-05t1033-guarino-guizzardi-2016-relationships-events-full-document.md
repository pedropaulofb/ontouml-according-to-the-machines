# Material

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source presents the earlier Guizzardi distinction in which material relations require an intervening individual, in contrast with formal relations that hold directly (p. 3).
- The associated modeling move is to introduce a relator for material relations as the intervening individual (p. 3).
- The paper then revises the classification by emphasizing descriptive relations: most extrinsic relations formerly labeled material are treated as relations that deserve reification because they hold in virtue of individual qualities of the relata (pp. 4-5).
- Material-relation content should therefore be read in this source through the broader truthmaking account of reified relationships as weak truthmakers and quality complexes (pp. 6, 8-9, 12).

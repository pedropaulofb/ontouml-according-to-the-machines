# Material

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, material relations are relations with ontological truthmakers. Externally dependent modes and relators are explicitly described as truthmakers for material relations, with externally dependent modes supporting one-sided cases and relators supporting double-sided or multi-sided cases.

The source also identifies `componentOf` as a material relation and describes other domain-level relations as derived material relations. This intermediate contribution is limited to the source's truthmaker-centered and dependence-centered characterization.

# Characterization

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source supports characterization-style modeling through the inherence relation between qualities and their bearers (pp. 6-7).
- It treats a particularized property or individual quality as an object-like entity that is existentially dependent on and inheres in its bearer (p. 6).
- When a descriptive property is reified as an individual quality, instances of the reified quality class inhere in instances of the original bearer class (p. 7).
- This contribution grounds Characterization as the relation by which a reified descriptive feature is tied to the entity whose property it qualifies, subject to later OntoUML-specific validation.

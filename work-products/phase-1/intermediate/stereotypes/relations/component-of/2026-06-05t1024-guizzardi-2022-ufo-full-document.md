# Component Of

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, `componentOf` is a relation between a functional part and a functional complex. It combines proper parthood with individual functional dependence under the relevant functional types.

The source also classifies `componentOf` as a material relation. Because it depends on special circumstances and functional-dependence configurations, the source does not treat functional parthood as unrestrictedly transitive. In the composition analysis, the relation is also used to explain generic dependence between a whole and replaceable functional parts.

# Quality

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

Section 8.2.2 characterizes quality universals as intrinsic moment universals that are typically not represented explicitly in the proposed conceptual modeling profile. Instead, the source treats quality universals as the basis for attribute functions that map instances of a characterized universal to points in a quality structure.

In this source-specific account, a quality universal characterizes another universal, and each corresponding individual quality inheres in a bearer. The source uses this account to ground the interpretation of UML attributes and datatypes: attributes represent attribute functions derived from quality universals, and datatypes represent the quality structures that serve as their codomains. This contribution should therefore be read as a Chapter 8 account of qualities as the ontological basis of attributes, not as an explicit Chapter 8 stereotype definition.

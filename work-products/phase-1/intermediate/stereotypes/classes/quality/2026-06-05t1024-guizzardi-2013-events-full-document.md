# Quality

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source places qualities under the broader UFO category of tropes. Tropes are endurants that existentially depend on bearers; the paper states that this trope category includes qualities as well as dispositions.

The paper treats qualities as particularized properties within the broader category of tropes. It also uses the related notion of a quality structure, or property value space, to organize the possible values associated with a property of a given type.

The source separates a property from its value space: the same property may be associated with alternative quality structures. This separation is used in the source to discuss temporal properties of events, whose values may be specified through different temporal structures.

The source also notes that events, like objects, can be predicated with qualitative characteristics. In this paper, however, a more detailed sub-theory of qualities and quality structures for events is explicitly left outside the presented fragment.

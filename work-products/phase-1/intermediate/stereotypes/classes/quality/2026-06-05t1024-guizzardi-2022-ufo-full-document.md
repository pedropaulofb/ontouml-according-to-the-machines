# Quality

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a quality is an intrinsic moment: an individualized intrinsic property existentially dependent on a bearer. Qualities are distinguished from modes because they can be directly projected into value spaces, called quality structures.

The source gives a formal account in which quality types are associated with quality structures, quality structures contain quales, and the `hasValue` relation connects each quality to a unique quale. The source also distinguishes simple and complex qualities through whether the quality bears further qualities.

# Quality

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to the supplied source, `«quality»` is an OntoUML 2 ultimate-sortal stereotype for classes whose instances are qualities (Section 4.1, p. 8). The paper characterizes qualities as individual moments that can be mapped to a quality space and that may change while preserving identity (Section 2, p. 3). In the revised profile, `«quality»` is one of the ultimate-sortal stereotypes used to state the ontological nature of a type's instances and to supply the identity principle inherited by its sortal subtypes (Section 4.1, pp. 8-9).

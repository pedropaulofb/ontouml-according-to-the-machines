# Quality

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source treats individual qualities as central reification units for descriptive properties and descriptive relations (pp. 5, 7-8).
- An individual quality can be the minimal weak truthmaker of a descriptive property; its qualitative value may vary over time while the quality maintains identity (p. 7).
- Descriptive relations depend on qualities of their relata, and reified relationships are constituted by sums or complexes of individual qualities (pp. 5, 8, 11).
- Reifying a descriptive attribute as an individual quality supports representation of the reified entity's own details, temporal behavior, and causal interactions; the paper treats this as a weak reification strategy rather than reification of each determinate property value (pp. 7-8).

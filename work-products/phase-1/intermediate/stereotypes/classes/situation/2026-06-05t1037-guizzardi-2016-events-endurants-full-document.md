# Situation

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source characterizes situations as states of the world in which endurants are characterized by bearing properties, including relational properties. Events are described as mappings from and to such situations: they connect pre-event and post-event situations and thereby represent change.

The source also uses situations in its causal account of events. Events can bring about situations, and situations brought about by events can trigger the occurrence of further events. This positions situation as the state-like correlate needed to describe event causation and change in the UFO account used by the source.

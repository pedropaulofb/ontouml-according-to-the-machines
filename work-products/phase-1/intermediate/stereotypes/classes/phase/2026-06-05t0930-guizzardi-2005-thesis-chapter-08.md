# Phase

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `phase` represents a phased-sortal phase: an anti-rigid and relationally independent universal defined as part of a partition of a substance sortal.

The source gives two central constraints for `phase`. First, because phases are anti-rigid universals, a `phase` cannot appear as a supertype of a rigid universal. Second, a phase partition of a substance sortal is represented by a UML GeneralizationSet that is both complete and disjoint, with `isCovering = true` and `isDisjoint = true`.

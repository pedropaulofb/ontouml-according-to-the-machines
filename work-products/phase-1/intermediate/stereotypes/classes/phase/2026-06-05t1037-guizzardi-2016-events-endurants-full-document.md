# Phase

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source characterizes phases as contingent, sortal types of endurants. An individual may enter or leave a phase without losing identity. Phases are distinguished from roles because phase membership depends on intrinsic properties rather than relational properties.

The source also uses phase partitions as connection points between structural OntoUML models and behavioral models of change. In this view, phases encode possible states in the life cycle of endurants, while events and conditions promote transitions between them. The source further distinguishes causally active and causally inactive phases of endurants in its pattern for relating endurants and events: in an active phase, particularized properties can be manifested through events; in an inactive phase, those properties can no longer be manifested and the relevant qualities become immutable.

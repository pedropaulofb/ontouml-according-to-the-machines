# Collective

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a collective is a mereologically complex substantial. Its unity condition is that its parts play the same role with respect to the whole.

The source formally places collectives as one of the disjoint categories partitioning substantials, alongside objects and quantities. It also treats collective kinds as kinds specialized according to the ontological nature of their instances.

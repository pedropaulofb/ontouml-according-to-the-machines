# Collective

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `collective` is introduced as a concrete specialization of Substance Sortal. The source characterizes a collective as a substance sortal whose instances are collectives: collections of complexes with a uniform structure.

The source assigns `collective` a meta-attribute `isExtensional`. When `isExtensional` is true, all parts of the collective are essential, and change or destruction of any part terminates the existence of that collective. Section 8.3.2 further uses this commitment when constraining `memberOf` relations: essential parthood for member-collection relations is supported only when the whole is an extensional collective.

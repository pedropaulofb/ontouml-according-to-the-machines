# Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `mixin` represents a semi-rigid non-sortal. The source characterizes a mixin as capturing properties that are essential to some of its instances and accidental to others.

The source places `mixin` under the general constraints for mixin classes. A mixin class is a non-sortal, cannot be a subclass of a sortal, cannot have direct instances, and must therefore be abstract. In addition, the profile constrains `mixin` so that it cannot have a `roleMixin` as a supertype.

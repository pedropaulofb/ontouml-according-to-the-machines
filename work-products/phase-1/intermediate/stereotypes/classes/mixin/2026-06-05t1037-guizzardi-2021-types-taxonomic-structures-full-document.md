# Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to the supplied source, `«mixin»` is the OntoUML 2 stereotype for semi-rigid non-sortals (Section 4.1, p. 10). The paper characterizes mixins as non-sortals representing properties shared by things of different kinds, where those properties are essential to some instances and accidental to others (Section 2, p. 4). In OntoUML 2, a mixin's instances may follow different identity principles and are not constrained to a specific ontological nature. The stereotype therefore supports cross-kind classification without treating the classification as uniformly rigid or uniformly anti-rigid for all instances (Section 3; Section 4.1, pp. 6-10).

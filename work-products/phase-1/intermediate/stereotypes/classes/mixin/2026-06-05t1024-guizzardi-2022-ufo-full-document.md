# Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a mixin is a semi-rigid non-sortal endurant type. It defines properties that are essential to some of its instances and accidental to others.

The source's formal taxonomy defines mixins by semi-rigidity and non-sortality. Thus, a mixin does not provide a uniform identity principle, and its instances are also classified through sortals that ultimately specialize kinds.

# Relator

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

- The source uses the relator as the reifying construct introduced in earlier Guizzardi work for material relations: material relations require an intervening individual, and the relator stands for that individual (p. 3).
- The paper reframes that earlier construct through truthmaking: a relationship is not a tuple, but an object-like entity in virtue of which a relation holds (pp. 1-3).
- The source-specific contribution for Relator is therefore provisional: relator-like reified relationships are treated as quality complexes or weak truthmakers for descriptive relations, able to maintain identity while their internal qualitative configuration changes over time (pp. 6, 8-9, 12).

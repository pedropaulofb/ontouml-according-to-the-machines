# Relator

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a relator is a moment, hence an existentially dependent endurant. It is characterized as an aggregation of qua individuals and is existentially dependent on multiple individuals, namely the bearers of the qua individuals that compose it.

The source connects relators to material relations: relators serve as truthmakers for double-sided or multi-sided material relations. Formally, every relator is founded on a unique event, has qua individuals as parts, and mediates at least two distinct endurants.

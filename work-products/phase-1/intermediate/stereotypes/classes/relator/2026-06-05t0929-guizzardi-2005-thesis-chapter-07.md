# Relator

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

Chapter 7 uses Relator as an aggregate of qua individuals that share a foundation and mediate related individuals. Qua individuals associated with a role stand in a parthood relationship with a unique relator in the scope of a material relation (section 7.3).

Because a relator consists of at least two distinct qua individuals, the source treats the qua individuals composing it as existentially dependent on each other. This supports the role-modeling account in which the role's relational specialization condition is analyzed through a relator-derived material relation (section 7.3).

Chapter 7 also states that a relator universal induces a material relation between the mediated universals. In the functional-parthood analysis, functional qua individuals sharing a foundation compose a relator, and that relator mediates the corresponding anti-rigid functional restrictions (section 7.4).

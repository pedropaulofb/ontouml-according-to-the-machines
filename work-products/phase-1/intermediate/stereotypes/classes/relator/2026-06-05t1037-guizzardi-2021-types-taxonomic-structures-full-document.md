# Relator

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to the supplied source, `«relator»` is an OntoUML 2 ultimate-sortal stereotype for classes whose instances are relators (Section 4.1, p. 8). The paper places relators under moments and characterizes them as existentially dependent individuals that connect entities. It also states that instances of a relator type are existentially dependent on at least two distinct entities and are typically composed of modes (Section 2, p. 3). In OntoUML 2, `«relator»` therefore identifies both the relator nature of the instances and the ultimate sortal that provides their identity principle.

# Role

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `role` represents a phased-sortal role: an anti-rigid and relationally dependent universal. As an anti-rigid universal, a `role` cannot appear as a supertype of a rigid universal.

Section 8.2.2 and table 8-2 refine the Chapter 8 treatment of `role` by relating it to relators and mediation. In the revised profile, every `role` class must be connected to an association end of a `mediation` relation. This refines the earlier condition that the role's relational restriction condition must have at least one associated instance.

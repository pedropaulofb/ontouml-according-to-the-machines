# Role

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a role is an anti-rigid sortal endurant type. A role classifies its instances contingently, and its classification condition is relational rather than intrinsic. The source contrasts roles with phases by placing both under anti-rigid sortals, but assigning roles to relational contingent classification conditions.

The source also distinguishes roles from role mixins. Roles apply to individuals governed by a single kind, whereas role mixins can cover individuals of different kinds and thus different identity principles.

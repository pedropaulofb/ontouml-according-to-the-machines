# Quantity

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a quantity is a substantial, not a quality value or measurement. The term is reserved for substantials that are maximally topologically self-connected portions of homeomerous matter.

The source formally places quantities in the partition of substantials, disjoint from objects and collectives. It also recognizes quantity kinds as kinds whose instances are quantities.

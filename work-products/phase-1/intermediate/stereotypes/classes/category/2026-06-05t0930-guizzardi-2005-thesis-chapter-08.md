# Category

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `category` represents a rigid and relationally independent mixin. The source characterizes it as a dispersive universal that aggregates essential properties common to different substance sortals.

The source treats `category` under the general constraints for mixin classes. A mixin class is a non-sortal and cannot be a subclass of a sortal; it also cannot have direct instances and must therefore be abstract. Additionally, a `category` cannot have a `roleMixin` as a supertype; in the profile, a `category` can only be subsumed by another `category` or by a `mixin`.

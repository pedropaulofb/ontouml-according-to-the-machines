# Category

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a category is a rigid non-sortal endurant type. It captures essential properties shared by instances that may belong to multiple kinds. Since it is non-sortal, it does not supply a uniform identity principle for its instances.

The source's formal taxonomy places categories among the leaf categories of endurant types and defines them by the conjunction of rigidity and non-sortality.

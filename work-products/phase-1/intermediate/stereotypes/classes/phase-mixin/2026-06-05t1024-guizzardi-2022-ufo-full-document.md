# Phase Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a phase mixin is an anti-rigid non-sortal endurant type. It defines contingent properties, and its instantiation is characterized by intrinsic contingent conditions.

As a non-sortal, a phase mixin can classify instances that are ultimately governed by different kinds. The source formally places phase mixins and role mixins together as the anti-rigid non-sortals, distinguishing them by the kind of contingent condition involved.

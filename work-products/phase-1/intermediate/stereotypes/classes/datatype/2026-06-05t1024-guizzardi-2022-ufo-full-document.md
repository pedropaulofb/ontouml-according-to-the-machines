# Datatype

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

This source gives a limited but direct OntoUML-specific contribution for datatypes. It states that UFO includes a view on datatypes as semantic reference structures and, in the OntoUML representation discussed in the property-change case, treats datatypes as the simplest way to represent quality structures.

Within this source, datatypes therefore support the representation of quality-structure/value-space content rather than functioning as endurant kinds. This contribution is intentionally limited to that representational role.

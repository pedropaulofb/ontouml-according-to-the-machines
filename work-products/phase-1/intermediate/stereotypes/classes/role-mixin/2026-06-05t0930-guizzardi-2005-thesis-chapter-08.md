# Role Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to section 8.1.2 and table 8-1, `roleMixin` represents an anti-rigid and externally dependent non-sortal. The source characterizes it as a dispersive universal aggregating properties common to different roles.

The source places `roleMixin` under the general constraints for mixin classes: it is a non-sortal, cannot be a subclass of a sortal, cannot have direct instances, and must be abstract. Section 8.2.2 and table 8-2 refine the relational-dependence condition by requiring every `roleMixin` class to be connected to an association end of a `mediation` relation.

# Role Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

According to the supplied source, `«roleMixin»` is the OntoUML 2 stereotype for externally dependent anti-rigid non-sortals (Section 4.1, p. 10). The paper characterizes role mixins as dispersive types that aggregate properties common to different roles (Section 2, p. 4). As non-sortals, role mixins do not supply a uniform identity principle; instead, their instances may follow different identity principles and are not constrained to one ontological nature. In the revised theory, this supports role-like, relationally dependent classifications across the boundaries of ultimate sortals (Section 4.1, p. 10).

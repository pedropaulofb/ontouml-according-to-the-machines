# Role Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

The source characterizes a role mixin as a contingent, relationally dependent mixin. Like a role, it depends on a relation for its instantiation and is not essential to its instances. Unlike a role, it is not a sortal: its instances may belong to multiple kinds.

This contribution supports the use of role mixin for types that capture relationally grounded, contingent classifications across heterogeneous kinds. The source positions role mixin among the types that apply to endurants.

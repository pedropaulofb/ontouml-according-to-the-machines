# Role Mixin

## Source-Specific Description Contribution

In this source, a role mixin is an anti-rigid non-sortal endurant type. It defines contingent properties whose instantiation depends on relational contingent conditions.

The source explicitly distinguishes role mixins from roles by kind coverage. Roles classify individuals of a single kind, whereas role mixins can classify individuals of different kinds, which means their instances may adhere to different identity principles.
